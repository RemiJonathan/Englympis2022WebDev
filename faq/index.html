Frequently Asked Questions

Permitted/Prohibited Objects

Can I bring my own food and drinks?

Yes, you can bring your own food and drinks. However, you are not allowed to bring alcohol.

Can I bring my own chair?

Yes, you can bring your own chair. However, you are not allowed to bring a tent.

Can I bring my own umbrella?

Yes, you can bring your own umbrella. However, you are not allowed to bring a drone.

Can I bring my own camera?

Yes, you can bring your own camera. However, you are not allowed to bring a selfie stick.

Access to the site

How do I get to the festival?

You can get to the festival by car, bus, or train. The festival is located at 1234 Potato Street, Potato City, Potato Province.

How do I get to the festival by car?

You can get to the festival by car by taking the Potato Highway. The festival is located at 1234 Potato Street, Potato City, Potato Province.

How do I get to the festival by bus?

You can get to the festival by bus by taking the Potato Bus. The festival is located at 1234 Potato Street, Potato City, Potato Province.

How do I get to the festival by train?

You can get to the festival by train by taking the Potato Train. The festival is located at 1234 Potato Street, Potato City, Potato Province.

Sale and delivery of tickets

How do I buy tickets?

You can buy tickets by visiting our website at www.potatocloudfest.com. You can also buy tickets at the festival site.

How do I buy tickets online?

You can buy tickets online by visiting our website at www.potatocloudfest.com.

How do I buy tickets at the festival site?

You can buy tickets at the festival site by visiting our booth at the festival site.
